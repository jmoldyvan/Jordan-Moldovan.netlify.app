About Me

I am a software engineer with a focus in Web Development and building Web Applications. I currently provide service as a freelance/free agent to those who desire a modern online presence. However I find a great deal of joy working with data on the backend. When I'm not grinding away, I am improving my programming abilities by regularly attending local tech meetups and trying my hand at Hackathons. Beyond coding experience I have a large history with work that requires meticulous data collection, for example, I worked as a Registered Behavioral Therapist for young adults with autism, additionally collaborating on a team for local water quality management. My passion now lies with investing in the value I create for clients. As well as taking care of my 5 year old dog Bowzer and occasionally modding digital games.

My Skills and Services

I dedicate myself to presenting my clients with completed projects that not only meet, but exceed, their initial vision. Knowing what to expect when we work together is critical - here is how I deliver success:

1. Consultation - It all begins with a conversation where we'll pinpoint what this project is all about.

2. Proposal - A detailed project proposal including a visual preview will be presented and a plan of action will be decided.

3. Creation - Clients receive regular updates regarding their project status while I create and test it.

4. Launch & Support - Peace of mind is delivered with the final project in the form of available ongoing support.
